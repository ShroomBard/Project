Fire Prevention & Safety at Home

Abstract

A fire can happen anywhere, at unexpected times. Thus, it is important to be weary and and prepared for it in your own home. However, it is also important to do your best to prevent the posibility of it happening. In this website, we will give 3 ways for you to lower the risk of a fire in your home.

3 Ways to Prevent Fires in Your Home

1.) Unplug Items Not In Use


Excess electricty flowing through electronincs in your home is a potential fire hazard. Especially, when such items are unobserved for a period of time. They can experience a surge or overheat and catch fire.

2.) Don't Leave Fires Unattended


When you start a fire, you always have to keep your eye on it. This applies to both indoor and outdoor flames. When you watch over your fire, you ensure that there is not a problem or that nothing is getting out of controll.

3.) Keep Flammable Items Away From Heat


Keep flammable items away from the heat so that fire won't spread to them. Things like hair, paper, fabrics, etc. should be kept away from fire.
